# Privacy Policy for Forkd

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Forkd ("the app") is operated by Luca Martinez.

## Data We Collect
- **Location**: Used only to find nearby restaurants. Never stored or shared.
- **Account info**: Email and name if you create an account (stored securely via Firebase).
- **Usage data**: Spin counts stored locally on your device.
- **Ad data**: Google AdMob may collect data to serve ads. See Google's privacy policy for details.

## Data We Do Not Collect
We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with third parties except as described above.
