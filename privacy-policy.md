# Privacy Policy for Forkd App

Last updated: March 28, 2026

## Data We Collect
- **Location**: Used only to find nearby restaurants. Never stored on our servers.
- **Email & Name**: Collected when you create an account, stored securely via Firebase.
- **Preferences**: Your liked, disliked, blocked, and favorited restaurants, stored in Firebase tied to your account.
- **Advertising Data**: Google AdMob collects device identifiers and usage data to serve ads. See Google's Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Sign In With Apple
If you use Sign in with Apple, we receive only the information Apple provides. Your email may be hidden by Apple's relay service.

## Data Sharing
We do not sell your data. Data is shared only with Firebase (Google) for authentication/storage and AdMob for advertising.
